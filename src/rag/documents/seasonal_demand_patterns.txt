## KEY UK TRADING EVENTS

### Public Holiday Demand Spikes
- Christmas Eve:      +180% perishables, buy 3+ days stock
- Christmas Day:      stores closed (plan for Dec 23-24 demand)
- Boxing Day:         -60% vs normal (most stores closed or reduced hours)
- Easter Friday:      +45% total, fish +40%, eggs +200%
- Easter Monday:      +20% family meals
- Bank Holidays:      +25% average, BBQ items +50% if sunny

### Events Calendar (demand uplift)
- Super Bowl Sunday (Feb):  no significant UK impact

- Father's Day (Jun):       BBQ/steak +30%
- Halloween (Oct 31):       confectionery +500%, pumpkins +400%

## DEMAND IMPACT OF LOCAL EVENTS

- Sporting events at local stadium: footfall -15% during match, +20% day before
- School holidays: family sizes +10%, snack items +25%
- Local festivals/events: BBQ items +40% if outdoor, footfall +30%
- Severe weather warning: panic buying — staples +50%, fresh -30% (footfall drops)

## CATEGORY INTERACTION EFFECTS

Heatwave cross-selling:
- BBQ meats up → salads up → beverages up → ice cream up
- Hot ready meals down, soups down, heavy pasta dishes down

Cold snap cross-selling:
- Soups up → bread up → butter up → comfort ready meals up
- Salads down, cold beverages down, fresh fish down (perceived as summer food)

Promotion halo effects:
- Discounting chicken breast increases prawn sales +8% (meal planning)
- Discounting salmon increases lemon +25%, dill +15% (meal accompaniments)
